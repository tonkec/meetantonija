Hello world

I am a developer who enjoys spending her evenings exploring new cool stuff on the web. The biggest joy I get in professional work is when I start project completely from scratch. I am CSS junky, I really care about CSS. I believe its importance is underestimated among developers. That is why I started Meetup group in Zagreb for CSS only.

I wrote my first line of code long time ago in the 90s. I was that kid in the neighbourhood who could install the latest Windows on any machine. I got serious about coding around 2015 when I got first job in coding.

Due to my introvertism I like to work in smaller teams where people are familiar and nice to me. I believe delivering a software then becomes more personal experience. Then I feel happy to invest my time and effort into software that I care about.
